- Influence of Bubbles on the Energy Conversion Efficiency of Electrochemical Reactors
- A. Angulo et al., Joule, 4, 555 (2020).

気泡と過電圧の関係がよくまとまっている．

| Overpotential Component | Typical Effect of Bubbles                                                                                                                         |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Activation              | attached bubbles increase the overpotential due to masking of the electrodes and decrease of the effective electrocatalytic area                  |
| Ohmic                   | attached and free bubbles increase the overpotential due to a blockage of the ion pathways available for current transport                        |
| Concentration           | bubbles may decrease the concentration overpotential by absorbing dissolved gas products and decreasing supersaturation levels in the electrolyte |
